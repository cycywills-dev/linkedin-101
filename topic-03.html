Section 02 · Profile Setup

Your profile photo

Profiles with a photo get 21× more views and 9× more connection requests.

✓ Great — Headshot
Clear face, looking at camera · neutral background · well-lit & sharp · smart-casual attire.
✓ Great — Framing
Dressed for your industry · confident posture · recent (2–3 yrs) · face fills 60–70% of frame.
✗ Avoid — Mistakes
Blurry / low-res · cropped from a group photo · sunglasses, hats, filters · no photo at all.

Section 02 · Profile Setup

Profile header anatomy

Six elements decide your first impression. Get every one right.

1
Banner (1584×396px)
Show your field or niche. Use Canva — search 'LinkedIn banner'.
2
Profile photo
Headshot, well-lit, plain background; face fills 60–70% of frame.
3
Name + verification
Real, full name. Add pronouns and the audio-name feature.
4
Headline (220 chars)
Not a job title — Role | Value Prop | Passion. Shows in search.
5
Followers & connections
Connections show trust, followers show reach. Aim for 500+.
6
Open to Work / Hiring
Toggle the green banner when looking; limit to recruiters only.

All-Star profile

The six sections that matter

Complete all six and LinkedIn surfaces you far more often in recruiter searches.

📷 Profile photo
Professional headshot, good lighting, plain background. Smile.
🌐 Background banner
Show your field or brand. Canva templates make it easy.
⭐ Headline
Role | Value Prop | Passion/Goal — not just a job title.
📖 About / summary
3–5 first-person sentences: who you are + what you want.
💼 Experience
Include internships, part-time jobs, volunteering, campus roles.
🏅 Skills
Add 5–10 relevant skills and ask peers to endorse you.
