Section 03 · Your Story

Your About section

Write in the first person. Tell your story in 3–5 punchy sentences.

1 Who you are
Open with your current role or focus and the value you bring.
2 What you've done
Highlight 2–3 concrete wins, projects, or experiences.
3 What you want next
End with your goal — the roles, people, or work you want.

💡 Keep it human. First person, short sentences, and a clear ask read far better than a formal third-person bio.

Key takeaways

  • ✓Headline = Role | Value Prop | Passion — it is what appears in search results.
  • ✓Replace vague lines like “Looking for opportunities” with specific value.
  • ✓Write your About section in the first person, in 3–5 punchy sentences.
  • ✓Structure it: who you are → what you've done → what you want next.
