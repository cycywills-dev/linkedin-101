Four principles of strategic networking

🔗 Personalise every request
Mention how you know them or why you want to connect. Generic requests get ignored.
💬 Value first, ask second
Comment on a post or congratulate a win before asking for anything.
📈 Target 2nd-degree connections
Shared mutuals create instant common ground and make outreach feel natural.
🔔 Follow target companies
Track openings and culture; engage with their posts to get noticed.
Who to connect with first

Classmates → Professors → Alumni → Past internship teammates → Speakers at events you attend.

Templates

The perfect connection message

Personalised requests are accepted far more often. Adapt these to your situation.

Peer / Classmate
“Hi [Name], we're both studying [field] at [University]. I've seen your work on [project] and would love to stay connected as we navigate post-grad life. Hope to keep in touch!”
Alumni Outreach
“Hi [Name], I'm a [year] student at [University] studying [major]. Your path from [University] to [Company] is really inspiring — I'd love to connect and learn from your journey. No pressure at all!”
Recruiter / Professional
“Hi [Name], I came across your profile while researching [Company]. I'm really interested in [role/field] and your work on [specific thing] stood out. I'd love to connect and follow along.”
